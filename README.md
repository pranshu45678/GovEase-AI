# GovEase AI 🇮🇳

An AI-powered multilingual government assistance platform that helps citizens access government services more easily.

## Features

✅ AI Chatbot for Government Queries  
✅ Voice Assistant (Speech-to-Text)  
✅ English, Hindi & Telugu Support  
✅ Smart Document Checklist  
✅ Government Document Simplifier  
✅ Fee & Processing Time Information  
✅ Official Government Service Links

## Tech Stack

- Python
- Streamlit
- OpenRouter AI
- Gnani/Vachana Speech-to-Text

## Problem Statement

Government information is often complex, difficult to understand, and scattered across multiple portals. GovEase AI simplifies access to government services through AI-powered guidance, voice interaction, and multilingual support.

## Future Scope

- OCR-based document reading
- AI document verification
- Personalized scheme recommendations
- Additional Indian language support
